About Us

Hello, and greetings! Welcome to the Angel Grace Foundation website!

The Angel Grace Foundation is about boosting autism awareness to parents of autistic children.

We’re empowering parents with autistic children with a central nexus of information to inform a parent with an autistic child. Here on this site, you will find articles, webpages, videos, and links to other resources, about autism and parenting a child with autism.

Our goal is to make a difference for parents and children. Helping, one parent, and one child at a time. We’re honored and proud to help parents and children, by raising autism awareness by providing knowledge, information, and resources.

On the Spectrum

Asperger. Asperger's Syndrome. Aspie. One the Spectrum. Atypical. High-functioning. All these words and phrases have one key meaning: autism.

Autism is not a new thing. The term autism first was used by psychiatrist Eugen Bleuler in 1908. Later in the 1940's Kanner and Asperger studied autistic children.

Yet, it was not until the 1980's that autism research entered the mainstream. Now in the 21st century there is a better understanding and more information available about autism in children.

For a parent with an autistic child there is more knowledge, information, and understanding to help and guide an autistic child than ever before.
