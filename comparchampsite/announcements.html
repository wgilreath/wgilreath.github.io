    E-book Available!

    May 2013 - Computer Architecture: A Minimalist Perspective is published as an e-book, available for purchase and download on the Springer website. Here is a fragment of the e-mail announcement from the publisher:


    We would like to inform you that your book Computer Architecture: A Minimalist Perspective has now been published as an eBook – being a part of the Springer Book Archives...

    Best wishes,
    Your Springer Book Archives Team



    Please visit and join the CAAMP Annoucement Group to be added to the e-mail announcement list of any updates or announcements in relation to the website or the book. The annoucement group is read-only not a forum, with no discussion or conversation.
