Dependable:
Looks for a way to continue, rather than looking for an opportunity to stop.  

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Use of Time:
Can be relied on to regularly show up at assigned work area. Is on time and ready to work. Reponsibly returns from breaks and lunches on time. Uses time wisely. Ephesians 6:7 Work with enthusiasm as though you were working for the Lord rather than for people.  

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Quality of Work:
Pursues accuracy and effectiveness. Attempts to be thorough and organized. Shows pride in work. Is neat and maintains a clean work area. Produces equal to their ability. Colossians 3:23 Whatever you do, work at with all of your heart, as working for the Lord, not for men.  

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Cooperation with Supervisor:
Responds to all supervision in a positive and constructive manner. Adapts well to changes in responsibilities. Is surrendered, humble, and respectful to authority. Phillipians 2:3-4 Humbly consider others better than yourselves...look not only to your own interests.  

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Relationships with Others:
Is willing to cooperate and is tactful and courteous. Works well with others. Willing to communicate. Resolves conflict appropriately. Exhibits traits of a servant's heart. Does not have to be the focus of attention. Understands teamwork. Philippians 2:2 ...then make my joy complete by being like-minded, having the same love, being one in spirit and purpose.   

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Initiative:
Completes work without being constantly reminded. Is creative and a problem solver. Is alert to the needs of surroundings and takes action. Shows developing leadership. Proverbs 20:6 Many a man proclaims his own loyalty. But who can find a trustworthy man?  

Work Site Supervisor Comments:
Safety/Health Practices Including Dress:
Is concerned with safety rules and use of appropriate protective equipment as directed by work supervisor. Respects the potential danger of tools and machinery. Dresses appropriately for the job and practices personal hygiene.
